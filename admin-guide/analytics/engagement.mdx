# Engagement Analytics

In a nutshell, the engagement analytics system tracks member activity levels, calculates engagement scores, and identifies members at risk of churning to help you maintain high member retention and satisfaction.

## Overview

The engagement analytics system provides comprehensive insights into member engagement levels and activity patterns. It calculates individual engagement scores, identifies high-performing members, and alerts you to members who may need attention to prevent churn.

Key capabilities:

- **Engagement Scoring**: Calculate individual member engagement scores
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify members at different risk levels
- **Performance Tracking**: Monitor top-performing members
- **Churn Prevention**: Alert on low-engagement members
- **Trend Analysis**: Track engagement changes over time
- **Actionable Insights**: Get specific recommendations for member retention

## Engagement Scoring System

### Score Calculation

The engagement score is calculated based on multiple factors:

- **Booking Frequency**: How often members book facilities
- **Spending Amount**: Total and average spending
- **Activity Recency**: Days since last booking
- **Area Diversity**: Variety of facilities used
- **Consistency**: Regular booking patterns

## Troubleshooting

**Issue**: Engagement scores not updating
**Solution**: Check that member activity data is being recorded properly

**Issue**: Low engagement alerts not showing
**Solution**: Ensure engagement criteria are set correctly

**Issue**: Scores seem inaccurate
**Solution**: Verify that all member activities are being tracked

**Issue**: Charts not loading
**Solution**: Refresh the page and check your internet connection

**Issue**: Filters not working
**Solution**: Clear all filters and reapply them one at a time

**Issue**: Performance is slow
**Solution**: Reduce the time range or limit the number of members being analyzed
