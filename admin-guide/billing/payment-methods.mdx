# Payment Methods

**In a nutshell:** Manage how members pay for their memberships and services, including credit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets, with secure processing and validation.

## What are Payment Methods?

Payment methods are the different ways members can pay for their memberships, classes, and services. This system handles everything from setting up accepted payment types to processing payments securely and tracking payment performance.

## Key Features

### **Payment Setup**

- **Configure accepted** payment methods for your club
- **Set up payment gateways** and processing systems
- **Manage payment fees** and pricing structures
- **Configure security settings** for payment processing

### **Member Payment Management**

- **Add payment methods** to member accounts
- **Validate payment information** before processing
- **Update payment details** when needed
- **Remove expired** or invalid payment methods

### **Secure Processing**

- **Process payments** securely through payment gateways
- **Handle payment failures** and retry logic
- **Manage payment disputes** and chargebacks
- **Maintain PCI compliance** for card payments

## Member Payment Methods

### Adding Payment Methods

1. Go to member's profile
2. Click **"Payment Methods"**
3. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
4. Select payment type
5. Enter payment details:
   - **Card Number**: Credit/debit card number
   - **Expiration Date**: Card expiration
   - **CVV**: Security code
   - **Billing Address**: Address for billing
   - **Account Details**: Bank account information
6. Verify payment method
7. Save payment method

### Payment Method Types

- **Primary**: Default payment method
- **Secondary**: Backup payment method
- **One-time**: Single use payment
- **Recurring**: For recurring payments
- **Corporate**: Business payment method
- **Guest**: Temporary payment method

### Payment Method Validation

- **Card Validation**: Verify card details
- **Address Verification**: Verify billing address
- **CVV Verification**: Verify security code
- **Account Verification**: Verify bank accounts
- **Expiration Check**: Check expiration dates
- **Fraud Detection**: Detect fraudulent payments

## Payment Processing

### Processing Payments

1. Select payment method
2. Enter payment amount
3. Add payment description
4. Process payment:
   - **Immediate Processing**: Process immediately
   - **Scheduled Processing**: Process at specific time
   - **Batch Processing**: Process with other payments
5. Confirm payment success
6. Send confirmation to member
