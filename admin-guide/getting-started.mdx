# Getting Started with 1Club Admin

**In a nutshell:** Welcome to 1Club! This guide will help you set up your account, understand the interface, and start managing your club effectively.

## What is 1Club Admin?

1Club Admin is your central command center for managing all aspects of your club or facility. From member management to billing, scheduling to analytics, everything you need to run your business is right here in one place.

## First Steps

### **Initial Login**

1. **Access the admin panel** at your organization's admin URL
2. **Sign in** with your credentials provided by your administrator
3. **Complete your profile** in Settings â†’ Profile if needed
4. **Explore the dashboard** to get familiar with the interface

### **Account Setup**

- **Update your profile** with current contact information
- **Set notification preferences** for email and SMS alerts
- **Configure your dashboard** to show the metrics you need most
- **Review your permissions** and access levels
