# Deal Management

**In a nutshell:** Create and manage deals, promotions, and special offers to attract new members and retain existing ones. Track deal performance and revenue impact.

## What is Deal Management?

Deal management helps you create and track special offers, promotions, and discounts for your club. Whether you're offering a new member discount, a seasonal promotion, or a special package deal, this system helps you manage the entire process from creation to completion.

## Key Features

### **Deal Creation**

- **Create custom deals** with specific terms
- **Set deal values** and discount amounts
- **Define deal duration** and expiration dates
- **Link deals to contacts** and memberships

### **Deal Tracking**

- **Track deal performance** and conversion rates
- **Monitor revenue impact** of each deal
- **View deal statistics** and analytics
- **Export deal data** for reporting

### **Deal Management**

- **Edit deal details** and terms
- **Activate or deactivate** deals
- **Duplicate deals** for similar offers
- **Archive completed** deals

## Creating Deals

### **Basic Deal Setup**

1. Go to **Marketing** â†’ **Deals**
2. Click **Add Deal**
3. **Enter deal name** and description
4. **Set deal value** and terms
5. **Choose deal type** and category
6. **Set expiration date** if applicable
7. **Save the deal**

### **Deal Information**

- **Deal Name** - Clear, descriptive title
- **Description** - Detailed terms and conditions
- **Value** - Discount amount or percentage
- **Type** - Fixed amount or percentage discount
- **Category** - Group deals by type
- **Status** - Active, inactive, or expired

### **Deal Terms**

- **Discount Type** - Fixed amount or percentage
- **Minimum Purchase** - Required spending amount
- **Maximum Discount** - Cap on discount amount
- **Valid For** - Specific memberships or services
- **Expiration Date** - When the deal expires

## Troubleshooting

### **Common Issues**

- **Deals not applying** - Check deal status and terms
- **Incorrect discounts** - Verify deal calculations
- **Expired deals** still showing - Update deal status
- **Member complaints** - Review deal terms and conditions

### **Deal Problems**

- **Deal not visible** - Check activation status
- **Wrong discount amount** - Verify deal settings
- **Deal expired early** - Check expiration date
- **Member can't use deal** - Verify eligibility criteria

## Getting Started

1. **Create your first deal** with a simple discount
2. **Test the deal** with a small group
3. **Monitor performance** and adjust as needed
4. **Expand to more** complex deal types
5. **Build a library** of successful deals

Deal management helps you attract new members and retain existing ones through strategic promotions. Start with simple deals and gradually build more complex offers as you learn what works best for your club.
