# Marketing & Deals Overview

**In a nutshell:** Attract new members, retain existing ones, and increase revenue through targeted promotions, special offers, and sales pipeline management.

## What is Marketing & Deals?

Marketing & Deals is your comprehensive system for growing your club's membership and revenue. It includes tools for creating special offers, managing sales opportunities, running marketing campaigns, and tracking performance to help you attract new members and keep existing ones engaged.

## Key Features

### **Deal Management**

- **Create special offers** and promotions for members
- **Manage discount codes** and promotional pricing
- **Track deal performance** and conversion rates
- **Set up seasonal** and limited-time offers

### **Sales Pipeline**

- **Track sales opportunities** and potential members
- **Manage leads** from various sources
- **Follow up on prospects** systematically
- **Convert leads** into paying members

### **Campaign Management**

- **Design marketing campaigns** across multiple channels
- **Target specific member** segments with personalized offers
- **Track campaign performance** and ROI
- **Automate follow-up** sequences and communications
