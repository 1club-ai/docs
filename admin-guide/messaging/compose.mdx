# Compose Message

**In a nutshell:** Create and send emails or SMS messages to your contacts. Use templates, schedule delivery, and track engagement all from one interface.

## Getting Started

### **Access the Compose Page**

1. Go to **Messaging** in the main menu
2. Click **Compose** to start a new message
3. Or click **Compose** from the message list page

### **Quick Message Flow**

1. **Select recipients** from your contact list
2. **Choose message type** (email or SMS)
3. **Write your content** or use a template
4. **Send immediately** or schedule for later

## Recipient Selection

### **Filter by Contact Type**

- **All Contacts** - See everyone in your database
- **Members** - Active club members only
- **Leads** - Potential members and prospects
- **Staff** - Your team members
- **Contacts** - General contact list

### **Select Recipients**

1. **Choose contact type** from the dropdown
2. **Search for specific contacts** in the search box
3. **Select individual contacts** from the filtered list
4. **Use "Select All"** to choose everyone in the current filter
5. **Clear selection** to start over

### **Bulk Selection Tips**

- **Filter first** by contact type for targeted messaging
- **Use "Select All"** for mass communications
- **Verify your list** before sending
- **Save time** by pre-filtering to the right audience

## Message Types

### **Email Messages**

- **Rich formatting** with images, links, and styling
- **HTML content** for professional appearance
- **Large character limits** for detailed messages
- **Attachment support** for documents and images

### **SMS Messages**

- **Plain text only** for maximum compatibility
- **Character limits** (typically 160 characters)
- **Quick delivery** for urgent communications
- **Mobile-optimized** for phone screens

## Content Creation

### **Subject Line**

- **Keep it clear** and descriptive
- **Avoid spam words** that trigger filters
- **Make it personal** when possible
- **Test different approaches** for better open rates

### **Message Content**

- **Use the rich text editor** for emails
- **Format with headings** and bullet points
- **Add images** and links for engagement
- **Keep SMS messages** short and direct

### **Template Integration**

1. **Select a template** from the dropdown
2. **Template content** automatically fills in
3. **Customize the content** as needed
4. **Save changes** before sending

## Scheduling Messages

### **Enable Scheduling**

1. **Toggle the "Schedule Message"** switch
2. **Choose date and time** for delivery
3. **Messages send automatically** at the scheduled time
4. **Edit or cancel** scheduled messages anytime

### **Scheduling Best Practices**

- **Schedule during business hours** for better engagement
- **Consider time zones** for global audiences
- **Test scheduling** with a small group first
- **Use scheduling** for promotional campaigns

## Message Actions

### **Send Immediately**

- **Click "Send"** to deliver right away
- **Message goes to all selected recipients**
- **Delivery status** updates in real-time
- **Cannot be undone** once sent

### **Save as Draft**

- **Click "Save Draft"** to save for later
- **Edit and send** when ready
- **Drafts appear** in your message list
- **Perfect for complex messages**

### **Schedule for Later**

- **Enable scheduling** and set time
- **Click "Schedule"** to queue the message
- **Messages send automatically** at the set time
- **Manage scheduled messages** from the message list

## Advanced Features

### **Reply to Messages**

- **Click "Reply"** on any received message
- **Original sender** becomes the recipient
- **Subject line** automatically prefixed with "Re:"
- **Maintain conversation thread** for context

### **Edit Existing Messages**

- **Click "Edit"** on draft messages
- **Modify content** and recipients
- **Update scheduling** if needed
- **Save changes** and send when ready

### **Message Preview**

- **See how your message looks** before sending
- **Preview on different devices** (desktop, mobile)
- **Check formatting** and links
- **Verify recipient count** and details

## Validation and Errors

### **Required Fields**

- **At least one recipient** must be selected
- **Subject line** cannot be empty
- **Message content** must have text
- **Scheduled messages** need a valid date/time

### **Common Issues**

- **No recipients selected** - Choose at least one contact
- **Empty subject** - Add a descriptive subject line
- **Empty content** - Write your message content
- **Invalid date** - Check your scheduling date/time

### **Error Handling**

- **Validation errors** appear below the relevant fields
- **Fix errors** before sending
- **Error messages** explain what needs to be corrected
- **Form saves progress** as you type

## Tips for Success

### **Message Content**

- **Write compelling subjects** that encourage opens
- **Use clear, concise language** for better readability
- **Include a call-to-action** when appropriate
- **Test with a small group** before mass sending

### **Recipient Management**

- **Segment your audience** for targeted messaging
- **Use contact types** to filter effectively
- **Verify recipient lists** before sending
- **Respect opt-out preferences** and privacy

### **Timing and Scheduling**

- **Send during peak hours** for better engagement
- **Consider your audience's timezone** for global reach
- **Use scheduling** for consistent communication
- **Plan campaigns** in advance for better results

The compose page is designed to be intuitive and powerful, giving you everything you need to create effective messages that reach the right people at the right time.
