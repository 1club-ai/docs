# Settings Overview

**In a nutshell:** Configure and customize your club management system to meet your specific needs, from basic club information to advanced integrations and user management.

## What are Settings?

Settings are the configuration options that allow you to customize your 1Club admin system for your specific club or facility. This includes everything from basic information like your club name and address to advanced features like user permissions and third-party integrations.

## Key Features

### **Profile Management**

- **Personal profile** - Your individual admin account settings
- **Club profile** - Basic information about your club or facility
- **Account preferences** - Notification settings and personal preferences
- **Security settings** - Password, two-factor authentication, and access control

### **Organization Settings**

- **Club information** - Name, address, contact details, and business hours
- **Branding** - Logo, colors, fonts, and visual identity
- **System configuration** - Default settings, time zones, and preferences
- **Business rules** - Policies, procedures, and operational settings

### **User Management**

- **Admin users** - Create and manage other admin accounts
- **Roles and permissions** - Control what each user can access and do
- **Access levels** - Different permission levels for different staff members
- **User activity** - Monitor who's doing what in the system
