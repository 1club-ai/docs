# Profile Settings

**In a nutshell:** Manage your personal admin account information, preferences, and security settings to customize your experience and keep your account secure.

## What are Profile Settings?

Profile settings let you manage your personal admin account information, including contact details, preferences, security settings, and account customization options. This is your personal space within the admin system.

## Key Features

### **Personal Information**

- **Name and contact** details
- **Profile photo** and avatar
- **Job title** and department
- **Contact information** and preferences
- **Account status** and permissions

### **Account Preferences**

- **Language and timezone** settings
- **Notification preferences** and frequency
- **Dashboard customization** and layout
- **Theme and appearance** options
- **Default settings** for various features

### **Security Settings**

- **Password management** and updates
- **Two-factor authentication** setup
- **Login history** and security logs
- **Session management** and timeouts
- **Account recovery** options

## Security Management

### **Password Security**

1. Go to **Security** section in profile
2. **Click "Change Password"**
3. **Enter current password** for verification
4. **Create new password** with strong criteria
5. **Confirm new password** and save

### **Two-Factor Authentication**

1. **Enable 2FA** in security settings
2. **Scan QR code** with authenticator app
3. **Enter verification code** to confirm
4. **Save backup codes** for recovery
5. **Test 2FA** with login attempt

### **Login Security**

- **View login history** and locations
- **Monitor active sessions** and devices
- **Log out** from other devices if needed
- **Set session timeout** preferences
- **Enable security alerts** for suspicious activity

## Best Practices

### **Information Security**

- **Use strong passwords** with mixed characters
- **Enable two-factor authentication** for extra security
- **Keep contact information** up to date
- **Regularly review** login history and activity
- **Log out** from shared or public computers

### **Profile Maintenance**

- **Update information** when it changes
- **Keep profile photo** current and professional
- **Review preferences** periodically
- **Test notification** settings to ensure they work
- **Backup important** account information

### **Account Monitoring**

- **Check login history** regularly for unusual activity
- **Monitor active sessions** and log out unused ones
- **Review permission changes** and access levels
- **Report suspicious activity** immediately
- **Keep recovery information** current

## Troubleshooting

### **Common Issues**

- **Can't update profile** - Check required fields and permissions
- **Photo not uploading** - Verify file size and format
- **Notifications not working** - Check email settings and spam filters
- **Password reset** - Use account recovery options
- **2FA problems** - Use backup codes or contact support

### **Account Problems**

- **Login issues** - Check credentials and account status
- **Permission errors** - Verify role assignments and access levels
- **Notification problems** - Review settings and contact information
- **Security concerns** - Check login history and enable additional security
- **Account locked** - Contact administrator for assistance
