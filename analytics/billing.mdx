In a nutshell, the billing analytics system provides comprehensive financial insights into your club's revenue streams, payment methods, invoice performance, and member billing behavior to help optimize your financial operations.

## Overview

The billing analytics system gives you detailed insights into your club's financial performance. You can track revenue trends, analyze payment methods, monitor invoice collection, and understand member billing patterns to make informed financial decisions.

Key capabilities:

- **Revenue Analysis**: Track total revenue and growth trends
- **Payment Method Analysis**: Understand how members prefer to pay
- **Invoice Metrics**: Monitor invoice collection and overdue amounts
- **Member Billing Behavior**: Analyze spending patterns and payment history
- **Financial Metrics**: Track profit margins and cash flow
- **Revenue Breakdown**: Categorize revenue by source

## Revenue Analysis

### Revenue Tracking

- **Total Revenue**: Combined revenue from all sources
- **Revenue Growth**: Track growth rates over time
- **Revenue Trends**: Identify patterns and seasonality
- **Revenue Forecasting**: Predict future revenue based on trends

### Revenue Sources

- **Booking Revenue**: Income from area and facility bookings
- **Membership Revenue**: Income from membership subscriptions
- **Other Revenue**: Income from additional services
- **Revenue Mix**: Percentage breakdown by source

### Revenue Metrics

- **Average Transaction Value**: Mean spending per transaction
- **Revenue Per Member**: Average revenue per active member
- **Revenue Per Area**: Revenue generated by each facility
- **Revenue Efficiency**: Revenue per hour of operation

## Payment Method Analysis

### Payment Method Performance

- **Credit Card**: Most common payment method
- **Debit Card**: Direct bank account payments

- **Cash**: Physical cash payments
- **Digital Wallet**: Mobile and online payments

### Payment Metrics

- **Transaction Volume**: Number of transactions per method
- **Success Rate**: Percentage of successful payments
- **Processing Time**: Average time to process payments
- **Average Amount**: Mean transaction value per method

### Payment Optimization

- **Method Popularity**: Identify preferred payment methods
- **Success Rate Analysis**: Optimize payment processing
- **Cost Analysis**: Compare processing costs
- **Member Preferences**: Understand payment habits

### Invoice Analysis

## Troubleshooting

**Issue**: Revenue data appears incorrect
**Solution**: Check that bookings have proper pricing set and invoices are generated

**Issue**: Payment method data missing
**Solution**: Ensure payment processing is properly configured

**Issue**: Invoice metrics not updating
**Solution**: Check that invoices are being created and statuses are updated

**Issue**: Charts not loading
**Solution**: Refresh the page and check your internet connection

**Issue**: Export not working
**Solution**: Some data can be exported - look for export buttons on individual charts

**Issue**: Performance is slow
**Solution**: Reduce the time range or remove some filters to improve loading speed
