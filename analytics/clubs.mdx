The **Clubs** category in **Analytics** rolls up activity, revenue, and resource utilization for each of your locations. It answers two questions: what is happening in the gym right now, and how have the numbers trended over a period you choose. Both dashboards read live from your operational data, so bookings, check-ins, and payments show up as they land.

## Overview

Open **Analytics** and pick the **Clubs** category to reach two dashboards:

- **Today's performance** (`/analytics/clubs/today`) - A same-day snapshot of bookings, revenue, active members, and check-ins, broken down by hour. It refreshes on its own every five minutes.
- **Performance trends** (`/analytics/clubs/historical`) - The same metrics plus resource utilization over a date range you pick, with daily trends and day-of-week patterns.

Both dashboards carry a **club filter** at the top. Leave it on **All** to see the whole organization, or pick one location to scope every card and chart to it. Numbers are counted in your organization's time zone (set under **Settings > Organization**), so "today" means today where the gym is, not where you are.

## Today's performance

This is the live operations view. It shows the current date and the timestamp of the last completed hour at the top, and re-queries every five minutes so you can leave it open on a screen at the front desk. It has a club filter but no date filter, because it is always scoped to today.

### KPI cards

The top rows summarize the day so far:

- **Bookings created** - Bookings made for today, regardless of status.
- **Revenue today** - Net revenue recognized today. This counts money actually taken in: it excludes voided transactions, reversals, and the portion of any sale paid from wallet credit rather than fresh payment.
- **Active members** - Distinct members who have a booking or a check-in today.
- **Check-ins** - Total check-ins recorded today.
- **Bookings confirmed** - Bookings for today currently in confirmed status.
- **Bookings cancelled** - Bookings for today that were cancelled.
- **Memberships created** - New memberships sold today.
- **Revenue from bookings** - The share of today's net revenue that came from bookings (as opposed to membership sales).

### Hourly trends chart

A combined chart plots **bookings created**, **revenue**, and **check-ins** hour by hour across the day. Use it to see when your day peaks. Hover any hour for exact figures, and click a series name in the legend to hide or show it.

<Note>
The Today view focuses on activity and revenue by hour. For utilization rates (class, area, and instructor occupancy), use **Performance trends** below. Utilization is computed over completed days, not the current in-progress hours.
</Note>

## Performance trends

This is the historical view. Set a date range in the filter bar (it defaults to the last 30 days) and, optionally, a single club. The dashboard loads once both a start and end date are set. As the range widens, the trend charts automatically group points by day, week, or month so the chart stays readable.

### KPI cards

- **Total bookings** - Bookings created in the range.
- **Total revenue** - Net revenue for the range, on the same real-money basis as the Today view.
- **Total memberships** - Memberships sold in the range.
- **Total check-ins** - Check-ins recorded in the range.
- **Average booking value** - Total revenue divided by total bookings.
- **Average class utilization** - Average share of class seats filled.
- **Average area utilization** - Average share of area operating hours booked.
- **Average instructor utilization** - Average share of instructor operating hours booked.

### How utilization is calculated

Utilization compares what was booked against what was available, expressed as a percentage:

- **Class utilization** - Booked seats divided by class capacity (the sum of each class's maximum capacity for the day).
- **Area utilization** - Booked hours divided by the area's available hours, where availability comes from the area's (or club's) operating hours.
- **Instructor utilization** - Booked instructor hours divided by the instructor's available hours from their operating hours.

Because availability is driven by operating hours, keeping **Resources > Areas** and instructor schedules accurate directly affects whether these percentages are meaningful.

### Charts

- **Daily trends** - Bookings, check-ins, and revenue over the range on one chart.
- **Utilization trends** - Class, area, and instructor utilization percentages over time, with revenue overlaid as a dashed line so you can see whether busier resources actually convert to income.
- **Day of week patterns** - Average bookings, revenue, and check-ins for each weekday, so you can tell a quiet Tuesday from a packed Saturday.

## Worked examples

### Comparing two padel locations

You run two padel venues and want to know which courts are underused. Open **Performance trends**, set the range to the last 90 days, and note the **average area utilization** with the club filter on **All**. Then switch the filter to each venue in turn. If the downtown site sits at 78% area utilization and the suburban site at 41%, the suburban courts have slack. Cross-check the **day of week patterns** chart per site: if the suburban venue only fills on weekends, the fix is midweek programming or off-peak pricing rather than more courts.

### Spotting a revenue dip early

A CrossFit box keeps **Today's performance** open at reception. Mid-afternoon the **revenue today** card is well below a normal day while **bookings created** looks normal. The **hourly trends** chart shows check-ins tracking as usual but revenue flat. That pattern points to unpaid or comped bookings rather than low attendance. Confirm it in the [bookings dashboard](/analytics/operations) or the booking audit, which highlights unpaid bookings.

### Checking capacity before adding a class

A yoga studio is considering a second morning reformer slot. On **Performance trends**, filter to the studio and read **average class utilization** alongside the **day of week patterns** bars. If mornings already run near capacity and weekday demand is even, the second slot is justified. If utilization is 55% and concentrated on two days, promote the existing class first.

## Tips and best practices

- **Start with All clubs, then drill in.** Read the organization-wide numbers first, then switch the club filter to a single location to explain an outlier.
- **Set the date range once.** Both charts on Performance trends share the range from the filter bar, so you only set it once per visit.
- **Leave Today open at the desk.** It refreshes every five minutes on its own, which makes it a good live board for front-of-house staff.
- **Fix operating hours before trusting utilization.** Utilization percentages depend on operating hours for areas and instructors. If they look wrong, verify the schedule under **Resources > Areas** first.
- **Use the legend to declutter.** On busy charts, click a series in the legend to hide it and focus on one metric.

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Likely cause | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Performance trends shows nothing | No date range is set | Pick both a start and end date in the filter bar. The dashboard only loads once the range is complete. |
| Utilization percentages look far too low or too high | Operating hours for areas or instructors are missing or wrong, so available capacity is miscalculated | Check and correct operating hours under **Resources > Areas** and on instructor schedules. |
| Revenue is lower than the cash you expected | Net revenue excludes voided transactions, reversals, and any part of a sale paid from wallet credit | This is expected. Wallet-funded amounts are not new money in. Reconcile against [Analytics > Billing](/analytics/billing). |
| Today's revenue looks stalled while check-ins keep rising | Bookings are being taken without payment or are comped | Review unpaid bookings in the booking audit under [Analytics > Operations](/analytics/operations). |
| A club is missing from the filter | Your account only has access to certain locations, or the club is inactive | Confirm your access and the club's status under [Reference > Organization and clubs](/reference/organization-clubs). |
| Today's numbers seem an hour or a day off | "Today" uses the organization's time zone, not your device's | Verify the time zone under **Settings > Organization**. |
