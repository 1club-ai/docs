The Marketing dashboard gives you an overview of pipeline, deals, and promotion performance in one place.

## Overview

Use the **Analytics** hub and open the **Marketing** category to access the **Marketing dashboard**: high-level metrics for pipeline value, deals, and promotions. Filter by date or segment as available to track marketing effectiveness.
