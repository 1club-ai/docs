**In a nutshell:** Members analytics cover member counts, attendance, progression, engagement, and balance so you can understand retention and usage.

## Overview

Use the **Analytics** hub and open the **Members** category to access:

- **Members dashboard** — Overview of member counts and key member metrics.
- **Attendance** — Check-ins and attendance over time.
- **Progression report** — Progress along progression tracks (e.g. levels or programs).
- **Engagement** — Engagement metrics and activity.
- **Balance report** — Member balances (e.g. credit or dues).

Filter by club, date range, or segment as available. Use these reports to spot trends and support member success.
