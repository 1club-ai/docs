**In a nutshell:** The analytics hub gives you dashboards and reports for clubs, members, operations, billing, and marketing so you can track performance and make data-driven decisions.

## How to access analytics

Open **Analytics** from the main admin menu. The hub lists all report categories; open a category to see today, historical, or detailed reports. Use filters (club, date range, etc.) to focus on the data you need.

## Report categories

- **[Clubs](/analytics/clubs)** — Today and historical dashboards for club-level metrics and utilization.
- **[Members](/analytics/members)** — Members dashboard, attendance, progression report, engagement, and balance report.
- **[Operations](/analytics/operations)** — Bookings dashboard, bookings monthly report, and classes dashboard.
- **[Billing](/analytics/billing)** — Billing dashboard, revenue report, transactions report, and invoices dashboard.
- **[Marketing](/analytics/marketing)** — Marketing dashboard for pipeline, deals, and campaign performance.

## Detailed reports (by category)

Within each category you can open specific reports:

- **Engagement** — [Engagement](/analytics/engagement) dashboard for member engagement.
- **Contacts** — [Contacts](/analytics/contacts) analytics for member and contact metrics.
- **Areas** — [Areas](/analytics/areas) usage and booking statistics by area.

## Capabilities

The analytics system combines data from bookings, members, areas, billing, and operations to provide actionable insights through interactive charts, tables, and summary cards:


- **Real-time dashboards** — Live data visualization with interactive charts and graphs.
- **Performance metrics** — Track key performance indicators across all areas.
- **Time-based analysis** — Compare performance across different time periods.
- **Filtering and segmentation** — Drill down by club, area type, date range, and more.
- **Multi-dimensional views** — Analyze data by club, area type, and member segments.

## Dashboard Components

### Summary Cards

The analytics dashboard displays key metrics at a glance:

- **Total Bookings**: Number of all bookings in the selected period
- **Total Revenue**: Financial performance with currency formatting
- **Total Contacts**: Number of members and contacts in your system
- **Average Utilization**: How efficiently your areas are being used
- **Total Areas**: Number of facilities available for booking
- **Active Contacts**: Members who have made bookings recently
- **Average Order Value**: Mean spending per booking
- **Contact Retention**: Percentage of contacts who remain active

## Key Metrics Explained

### Utilization Rate

- **Definition**: Percentage of available time that areas are booked
- **Calculation**: (Booked Hours / Available Hours) × 100
- **Target**: Higher utilization indicates better space efficiency
- **Benchmark**: 60-80% is typically considered good utilization

### Revenue Per Hour

- **Definition**: Average revenue generated per hour of booking
- **Calculation**: Total Revenue / Total Booked Hours
- **Use**: Helps optimize pricing strategies
- **Comparison**: Compare across different areas and time periods

### Contact Retention

- **Definition**: Percentage of contacts who remain active
- **Calculation**: (Active Contacts / Total Contacts) × 100
- **Importance**: Indicates member satisfaction and engagement
- **Target**: Higher retention means better member loyalty

### Average Order Value

- **Definition**: Mean spending per booking
- **Calculation**: Total Revenue / Total Bookings
- **Use**: Helps understand spending patterns
- **Optimization**: Identify opportunities to increase spending

## Troubleshooting

**Issue**: Charts not loading
**Solution**: Refresh the page and check your internet connection

**Issue**: Data appears outdated
**Solution**: Analytics update in real-time, but may take a few minutes for new data

**Issue**: Filters not working
**Solution**: Clear all filters and reapply them one at a time

**Issue**: Export not available
**Solution**: Some chart data can be exported - look for export buttons on individual charts

**Issue**: Performance is slow
**Solution**: Reduce the time range or remove some filters to improve loading speed

**Issue**: Missing data in charts
**Solution**: Check that you have bookings and areas set up in your system
