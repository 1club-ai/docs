<Update label="2026-07-24" description="Anonymous check-ins and bookings now work for managers and front desk" tags={["Fixed","Operations"]}>
  ## Anonymous check-ins and bookings now work for managers and front desk

  When a booking policy allows anonymous check-ins or anonymous bookings, managers and front-desk staff can now use them from the admin dashboard. Previously the check-in and booking dialogs still required selecting a contact for everyone except admins.

  Booking policies can allow anonymous check-ins and anonymous bookings — entries
  recorded without picking a member. Until now, only admins could actually use
  them: for managers and front-desk staff the check-in and booking dialogs kept
  the contact field required even when the active policy allowed going without
  one.

  The dialogs now correctly recognize the policy for any staff member who can
  create check-ins or bookings, so managers and front desk get the same
  anonymous and walk-in options admins see. No configuration change is needed —
  existing booking policies apply as-is.

  [Learn more](/schedule)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-07-20" description="Social booking links" tags={["New","Marketing"]}>
  ## Social booking links

  Generate tagged booking links for your social channels and see which platforms drive bookings.

  Turn your social posts into trackable booking links. Pick a destination and a
  platform such as Instagram, Facebook, TikTok or YouTube, choose whether the post
  is organic or paid, and optionally name a campaign. 1Club builds a ready-to-share
  link with the right UTM tags attached, and you can add your own custom sources too.
  When someone books after following one of these links, the booking is attributed
  to that source, so your bookings analytics can show which channels and campaigns
  actually bring members in.

  [Learn more](/settings/integrations/social-booking)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-07-09" description="Events and ticketing" tags={["New","Highlight","Bookings"]}>
  ## Events and ticketing

  Create ticketed events, sell multiple ticket types, and check attendees in.

  You can now run events end to end. Set up an event with one or more ticket types,
  choose whether attendees pay online or on arrival, and let people buy several
  tickets in a single order across the portal, website, marketplace and mobile app.
  Live events reserve their area and instructor on the schedule so they never
  double book, and staff can check attendees in and collect payment from the
  attendee list. There is even an AI assistant to help draft an event.

  [Learn more](/operations/schedule)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-06-24" description="Instructor pay rates and shifts" tags={["New","Highlight","Operations"]}>
  ## Instructor pay rates and shifts

  Set how each instructor is paid and plan their shifts, with earnings visible to both of you.

  Managing instructors now covers both pay and time. Define how each instructor is
  paid, as a fixed amount or a percentage, and choose whether they are paid only for
  paid bookings and whether the percentage is on gross or net revenue. Instructors
  get an earnings report with a monthly breakdown and a compensation card on their
  dashboard. Scheduling is built around shifts too, with rotations and roster merged
  into one view and coverage shown right on the scheduler calendar.

  [Learn more](/operations/instructors)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-06-24" description="Member mobile app" tags={["New","Highlight","Platform"]}>
  ## Member mobile app

  Members can now book, pay and manage their membership from a native iOS and Android app.

  The 1club member app is here for iOS and Android. Members can browse the schedule
  and book classes and courts, buy plans and pay with Apple Pay, manage memberships,
  and chat with your club from a built-in inbox. Push notifications keep them up to
  date on bookings and messages, sessions stay signed in securely between visits,
  and the whole app follows the member's language and your club's time zone.

  [Learn more](/members/overview)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-06-23" description="myPOS payments" tags={["New","Highlight","Billing"]}>
  ## myPOS payments

  Take payments through myPOS, including shareable payment links for memberships and orders.

  1club now integrates with myPOS as a payment provider. Clubs can connect myPOS,
  switch between test and live mode, and collect payment for memberships and orders
  through a hosted checkout. You can also send a shareable payment link so a member
  can pay from anywhere, with the checkout shown in the member's own language.

  [Learn more](/settings/integrations/mypos)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-06-04" description="Automations" tags={["New","Highlight","Marketing"]}>
  ## Automations

  Set up automated member messages from ready-made blueprints, with suggestions as you work.

  Automations replace campaigns with a simpler, always-on way to reach members.
  Start from suggested blueprints tailored to the page you are on, insert member

  chosen channels. Built-in automations, such as a review request and a membership

  and wording of each or switch it off.
