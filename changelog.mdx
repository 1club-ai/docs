<Update label="2026-07-23" description="Import your product catalog from a spreadsheet" tags={["New","Billing"]}>
  ## Import your product catalog from a spreadsheet

  You can now bulk-import products from a CSV, including price, VAT rate, barcode, category, cost, and opening stock for a chosen location.

  The data import tool now supports products. Export your existing product list
  to CSV and upload it the same way you import members or memberships - the
  importer detects the columns (including Bulgarian headers like "Име артикул",
  "Продажна цена", "ДДС %" and "Количество") and maps them for you.

  Each imported product can carry its own VAT rate. That rate is created once and
  then used at the point of sale, so a product is always taxed at the correct rate
  regardless of its revenue account. Products also gained a cost price (for margin
  reporting) and a product code (SKU) alongside the existing barcode.

  If your file has a quantity column, choose a location during the import and those
  quantities are recorded as opening stock for that location. Products without a
  quantity are imported without stock. Duplicate products (matched by barcode, then
  name) are skipped so you can safely re-run an import.
</Update>

<Update label="2026-07-22" description="Cash registry & expense management" tags={["New","Highlight","Billing"]}>
  ## Cash registry & expense management

  Track the cash in your drawer, record expenses, and see where the money goes — cash payments are captured automatically.

  You can now manage cash and expenses right inside 1club. Turn on the new
  Cash module in Settings and each club gets a cash drawer that always knows
  how much cash it should hold.

  - Cash payments you take for classes, memberships, and products are added to
    the drawer automatically — no double entry.
  - Record expenses (rent, instructor pay, supplies, water delivery, and more)
    against editable categories, with an optional receipt photo. Non-cash
    expenses like card or bank transfer are tracked for reporting without
    touching the drawer.
  - Add or remove cash for anything else (floats, deposits, pay-outs).
  - Optionally count the drawer at any time — 1club compares your count to the
    expected balance and records any over/short so the books match reality.
  - An overview dashboard shows cash on hand per club, cash in vs out over time,
    and expenses by category.

  Every entry is kept as a permanent record; corrections are made with a
  reversing entry rather than editing history, so the trail is always auditable.
</Update>

<Update label="2026-07-20" description="Social booking links" tags={["New","Marketing"]}>
  ## Social booking links

  Generate tagged booking links for your social channels and see which platforms drive bookings.

  Turn your social posts into trackable booking links. Pick a destination and a
  platform such as Instagram, Facebook, TikTok or YouTube, choose whether the post
  is organic or paid, and optionally name a campaign. 1Club builds a ready-to-share
  link with the right UTM tags attached, and you can add your own custom sources too.
  When someone books after following one of these links, the booking is attributed
  to that source, so your bookings analytics can show which channels and campaigns
  actually bring members in.

  [Learn more](/settings/integrations/social-booking)
</Update>

  ## Buy packages and products through the signup form

  Signup/purchase forms can now sell packages and add-on products end to end — the buyer checks out and pays through the standard flow and gets an order confirmation email.

  Purchase forms that include packages or add-on products now complete the
  sale: after signing up, the buyer is taken through the standard order
  checkout and pays the same way as anywhere else on the platform (card or

<Update label="2026-07-10" description="Real-time availability and webhooks for channel partners" tags={["New","Highlight","Platform"]}>
  ## Real-time availability and webhooks for channel partners

  Partner platforms can now read a whole club's open slots in one call and receive webhooks the moment a court's availability changes, so their calendars stay live.

  Partners selling your courts through the channel integration can now keep a
  truly live calendar.

  The availability API returns ready-made open slots — with remaining capacity
  and the club's timezone — for a single court or a whole club in one request,
  so partners no longer have to stitch together operating hours and busy
  intervals themselves.

  New availability webhooks push a signed event within seconds whenever a court
  is booked, freed, or blocked on any channel — your own tools, another partner,
  a class, or an event. Partners register an endpoint under Settings → APIs and
  MCP, verify the signature, and re-pull the affected slots. A reconciliation
  feed lets them catch up exactly after any missed delivery, so availability
  never drifts and double-bookings are still impossible.
</Update>

<Update label="2026-07-10" description="Self-service Check-in App" tags={["New","Highlight","Operations"]}>
  ## Self-service Check-in App

  Members can now check themselves into classes or the gym from a dedicated self-service screen at the club.

  A new touch-first Check-in App runs on a tablet or screen at your club, next to
  the existing kiosk. Members find themselves by name (or with their PIN, when
  enabled) and are checked in instantly - a green screen confirms the check-in,
  an amber screen means they are in but a payment is still due, and a red screen
  sends them to the front desk.

  - Members with an upcoming booking are checked in the moment they identify
    themselves.
  - Without a booking, members pick from the classes starting in the next hour;
    the club's booking policy decides whether their membership covers it or an
    unpaid payment is recorded for the front desk.
  - Choose the journey per club: identify first, or pick a class first and tap
    your name on the class list.
  - Open Gym mode checks members in on their general membership with no class
    list at all.

  Configure everything under Settings, Member Experience, Check-in App: enable it
  per club, copy the device link, toggle PIN identification and Open Gym mode,
  and drag to reorder the user journey.
</Update>
