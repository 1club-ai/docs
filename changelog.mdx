<Update label="2026-07-09" description="Recurring bookings" tags={["Major","Bookings"]}>
  ## Recurring bookings

  Members can now create recurring bookings.

  Set a booking to repeat on a weekly or monthly schedule instead of adding each
  session by hand. Recurring bookings respect area availability and membership
  allowances, and the whole series can be rescheduled or cancelled at once.

  [Learn more](/operations/bookings)
</Update>

  ## Bulk membership import
