Document signatures tracks which members have signed required documents (waivers, terms, policies) so you can see completion status and access signed copies for compliance.

## Overview

Open **Content > Document signatures** to see a list of signature records: which document was signed, by whom, when, and the version of the document at the time. Signatures are collected through the member portal or the public signup flow whenever a document rule requires them.

Open a row to see the signer, the document version, and the timestamp.
