"Content" in these docs covers everything your members and visitors see or sign: your public website, standalone content items (posts, FAQs, how-to guides, reusable blocks), your media library, member reviews, and records of documents members have signed. There is no single **Content** menu in the admin. These features live as tabs under **Marketing**, with signed-document records under **Members**. This page points you to each one.

## Where these features live

| Feature | In the admin | Docs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Public website | **Marketing > Website** | [Website builder](/content/website) |
| Content items | **Marketing > Content** | [Content items](/content/documents) |
| Media library | **Marketing > Media** | [Media](/content/media) |
| Reviews | **Marketing > Reviews** | [Reviews](/content/reviews) |
| Signed-document records | **Members > Document signatures** | [Document signatures](/content/document-signatures) |

## What each one does

The [website builder](/content/website) under **Marketing > Website** is the editor for your public site. Pages are composed of sections such as hero, plans, classes, contact form, and gallery, many of which pull live data from your gym (plans, classes, instructors, locations, reviews). Edits live in a draft and go live when you publish a release.

[Content items](/content/documents) under **Marketing > Content** are the standalone pieces you write yourself. There are four types: **posts** for news and blog entries, **how-to guides** for instructional content, **FAQs** for question and answer pairs, and **blocks** for reusable chunks of copy you reference from website sections. Each item has its own slug, language variants, and SEO fields.

[Media](/content/media) under **Marketing > Media** is the shared library for images and other files. Anything you upload here is available to website sections, content items, and message templates.

[Reviews](/content/reviews) under **Marketing > Reviews** is the moderation list for member reviews and any reviews synced from Google Business Profile.

[Document signatures](/content/document-signatures) under **Members > Document signatures** lists records of waivers, terms, and policies signed by members through the portal or signup flow. The signable documents themselves are defined under **Settings > Document rules**.

## Choosing between the website and content items

If you want to ship a brand-new page on your public site, work in the [website builder](/content/website). If you want to write a single article that can be referenced from a website section (or appear in the **News**, **FAQ**, or **How-to** section), create a [content item](/content/documents).

Reusable copy you do not want to retype on multiple pages (legal blurbs, "about us" paragraphs) belongs as a **Block** content item, which the website builder's `Block` section can pull in by slug.
