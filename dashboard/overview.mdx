**In a nutshell:** Your central command center that shows key metrics, recent activity, and provides quick access to the most important features of your club management system.

## What is the Dashboard?

The dashboard is your main workspace in the 1Club admin interface. It gives you an instant overview of your club's performance, recent activity, and quick access to the features you use most often. Think of it as your mission control for running your club.

## Customizing Your Dashboard

### **Adding Widgets**

1. Click **"Customize Dashboard"** button
2. **Select widgets** from available options
3. **Drag and drop** to arrange them
4. **Save configuration** to apply changes

### **Removing Widgets**

1. Click **"Customize Dashboard"** button
2. **Click remove** on unwanted widgets
3. **Confirm removal** in the dialog
4. **Save changes** to update dashboard

### **Reordering Widgets**

1. Click **"Customize Dashboard"** button
2. **Drag widgets** to new positions
3. **Release to drop** in new location
4. **Save layout** to apply changes

### **Widget Settings**

- **Size options** - Small, medium, or large
- **Refresh rate** - How often data updates
- **Visibility** - Show or hide specific widgets
- **Data filters** - Customize what information displays

## Troubleshooting

### **Common Issues**

- **Widget not loading** - Check internet connection and refresh page
- **Data not updating** - Verify widget refresh settings and permissions
- **Slow performance** - Reduce number of widgets or increase refresh intervals
- **Missing information** - Check data permissions and filter settings

### **Dashboard Problems**

- **Layout not saving** - Ensure you click "Save" after making changes
- **Widgets not responding** - Try refreshing the page or clearing browser cache
- **Incorrect data** - Verify data sources and check for system updates
- **Access issues** - Confirm your user role has appropriate permissions
