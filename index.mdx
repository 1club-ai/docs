## Welcome

1Club is an AI-native gym and club management platform. Use the admin dashboard to manage members, bookings, billing, marketing, and your club's operations in one place.

## What is 1Club Admin?

1Club Admin is your central command center for managing all aspects of your club or facility. From member management to billing, scheduling to analytics, everything you need to run your business is in one place.

## First steps

### Initial login

1. **Access the admin panel** at your organization's admin URL.
2. **Sign in** with your credentials provided by your administrator.
3. **Complete your profile** in **Settings → Profile** if needed.
4. **Explore the dashboard** to get familiar with the interface.

### Account setup

- **Update your profile** with current contact information.
- **Set notification preferences** for email and SMS alerts.
- **Configure your dashboard** to show the metrics you need most.
- **Review your permissions** and access levels.

## Quick start guide

### Day 1: Basic setup

1. Complete your profile with accurate information.
2. Explore the dashboard and customize widgets.
3. Review member data and understand current status.
4. Check billing settings and payment methods.

### Week 1: Core operations

1. Set up areas and facilities for bookings.
2. Create membership plans and pricing.
3. Configure class schedules and instructor assignments.
4. Test the booking system with sample data.

### Month 1: Full system

1. Implement automated billing and payment processing.
2. Set up messaging templates for member communication.
3. Configure analytics and reporting dashboards.
4. Train staff on system usage and best practices.
