The Business audit generates an AI report that scores your club's online business profile, review sentiment, website performance, and AI discoverability - with actionable recommendations to improve.

## Overview

Open **Marketing → Business audit** to generate or review reports for your club. The report combines public information about your business with website and PageSpeed checks into a single scored summary, so you can see at a glance where to focus your marketing efforts.

## Generating a report

1. Navigate to **Marketing → Business audit**
2. The business name is pre-filled from your organization (and city, if set)
3. Adjust the business name if needed so it matches your public listing
4. Click **Generate** - you'll be taken to the public audit page where the report is built
5. Once generated, the report is listed back in the admin under the business audit page

## What the report scores

Each report produces an overall score plus several sub-scores:

- **Reviews** - Sentiment and volume of public reviews
- **Business info** - Whether your public listing has all expected fields
- **Completeness** - Coverage of business profile fields
- **Accuracy** - Consistency of business data across sources
- **Profile content** - Quality of photos, descriptions, and other profile assets
- **AI discoverability** - How well your business surfaces to AI assistants and search
- **PageSpeed: Performance / Accessibility / Best Practices / SEO** - Website performance against Google PageSpeed metrics

Scores are color-coded: green for ≥ 80, amber for ≥ 60, red below 60.

## Reviewing past reports

The page lists every report generated for your organization, with the business name, date created, and overall score. Click **View Report** to open the full report in a new tab.

## Tips & best practices

- **Run periodically** - Re-run the audit each quarter (or after a website change) to track progress
- **Match your public name** - Use the same business name your members see on Google so the audit pulls the right profile
- **Action the recommendations** - Each report ends with concrete actions; share them with whoever owns marketing or your website
