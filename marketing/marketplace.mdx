The 1club marketplace at [1club.me](https://1club.me) is a public directory where people discover and explore gyms. A published listing shows your gym's details, photos, amenities, and location to anyone browsing the marketplace - no account required.

You manage your listing from **Marketing > Marketplace** in the admin dashboard.

## Why list your gym

- **Reach new members** - your gym becomes visible to everyone browsing the marketplace.
- **Showcase your facilities** - photos, amenities, and available facilities appear on your public profile.
- **Get discovered** - visitors find gyms through search and filters (location, sports, amenities).

## Get your gym listed

1. Open **Marketing > Marketplace** and click **Start now**. If your organization has more than one gym, pick which one to list.
2. The guided setup wizard walks you through the listing profile: location, photos and contact details, description, and amenities. Everything already on file is prefilled.
3. Confirm the final step to submit your application for review.

Your application lands in an **In review** state. The 1club team reviews every application before publishing; you'll see the status on the Marketplace page, and the listing goes live once it's approved.

## Profile requirements

A gym can only be submitted when its profile is complete. The required fields are:

- Address
- Phone number
- Email
- Description
- Location (city and map pin)

Photos are optional but strongly recommended - listings with photos get noticed. If something is missing, the Marketplace page tells you exactly which fields to complete.

## Listing statuses

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **In review** | Submitted and waiting for approval by the 1club team. |
| **Published** | Live on the marketplace and shown in search and browse. |
| **Published (unlisted)** | Reachable by direct link, but hidden from marketplace search and browsing. |
| **Unpublished** | Not visible on the marketplace. |

## Managing a published listing

Once published, the Marketplace page lists each gym with its current status:

- **View listing** opens your public page on 1club.me.
- **Unpublish** takes the listing off the marketplace immediately - no review needed. To relist, submit the gym again and it goes back through review.
- Organizations with multiple gyms manage each listing separately; gyms without a listing can be submitted from the same page.
