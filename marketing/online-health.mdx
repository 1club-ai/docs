**In a nutshell:** The Online health dashboard helps you track how your club appears online—including reviews, business profiles, and engagement—so you can improve visibility and reputation.

## Overview

Use **Marketing → Online health** to see a summary of your club's online presence. This can include connection status with Google Business Profile, review metrics, and suggestions to improve your listing and engagement.

## Key features

- **Profile status** — See whether your Google Business Profile (or other profiles) is connected and up to date.
- **Reviews** — Overview of ratings and review volume.
- **Recommendations** — Actions to improve your online presence.
