The marketplace at **1club.me** is the public directory for the 1Club network. People search for gyms, classes, and sports by location and activity, browse a gym's profile, and get pointed to that gym's [member portal](/member-experience/member-portal) to actually book or join. It is a discovery front door: a way for new members to find your gym, distinct from your own [website](/content/website), which you control and brand yourself.

Opt your gym in and manage how it appears under **Settings > Marketplace listing**.

## What visitors can do

- **Search and browse** gyms by location, distance, and sport or activity, as a list or on a map.
- **Open a gym profile** to see its location, hours, photos, the class schedule, available plans, instructors, and reviews.
- **Get routed to book or join** - when a visitor chooses to book a class or buy a plan, the marketplace hands them to that gym's portal to sign in and complete the transaction.

Because checkout happens in your portal, your [booking policies](/operations/booking-policies), [plans](/billing/plans), and [payment methods](/settings/payment-methods) all apply exactly as they do for members who came in directly.

## For members across the network

A person with a network account can sign in on 1club.me and see their memberships and bookings across every 1Club gym they belong to in one place, then jump to the relevant gym's portal to manage each one. This is the cross-gym view; day-to-day booking, paying, and check-in still happen in each gym's own portal, app, or kiosk.

## Marketplace vs. your website

Both put your gym in front of the public, but they are different tools:

- **Your [website](/content/website)** is the site you design and control - your domain, your pages, your copy - built in the admin.
- **The marketplace** is 1Club's shared directory. You control your listing, but the surrounding experience (search, discovery, cross-gym profiles) is the network's.

Use your website as your primary online presence and the marketplace as an extra discovery channel that feeds new members into your portal.
