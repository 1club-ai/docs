Most of this help center describes the admin dashboard you use to run your gym. This section covers the other side: the apps your **members** use. Everything a member can do maps back to something you configure in the admin, so each page here links to the settings that control it.

## The four member channels

  <Card title="Member portal" icon="globe" href="/member-experience/member-portal">
    The web app where members sign in to book classes and facilities, buy plans, pay, sign documents, and manage their account. Runs on your own subdomain or custom domain.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mobile app" icon="mobile-screen" href="/member-experience/mobile-app">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Marketplace" icon="magnifying-glass" href="/member-experience/marketplace">
    The public 1club.me directory where people discover gyms across the network and get routed to your portal to book or join.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How the channels fit together

The portal and mobile app are the two full member surfaces - a member can do almost everything on either one, and the account is shared. The kiosk is an on-site companion for walk-up check-in and quick purchases. The marketplace is a discovery front door: it lists your gym publicly and hands members off to your portal to complete a booking or purchase.

All four read from the same organization data, so your plans, classes, instructors, areas, and branding stay consistent across them.

| Channel | Where it runs | Sign-in | Book | Pay | Check in |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Member portal | Your subdomain / custom domain | Yes | Yes | Yes | QR in browser |
| Mobile app | App Store / Google Play | Yes | Yes | Yes | QR card |
| Kiosk | Front-desk touchscreen | No member login | No | Yes (plans, day passes) | Yes |
| Marketplace | 1club.me | Yes (network account) | Redirects to portal | Redirects to portal | No |

<Note>
Every booking records the channel it came from (`portal`, `app`, `kiosk`, `network`, `website`, `admin`, and others). You can see this **source** on the [booking detail page](/operations/bookings).
</Note>

## What you configure in the admin

Each member channel is turned on and styled from **Settings**:

- **Branding** sets the logo, colors, and fonts that all member channels inherit. See [Settings overview](/settings/overview).
- **Member portal** enables the portal and sets its subdomain or custom domain.
- **Mobile app**, **Kiosk app**, and **Marketplace listing** each have their own settings entry to enable and configure the channel.
- **Booking policies** decide when payment is collected and how cancellations refund - the same rules apply whether a member books in the portal, app, or at the kiosk. See [Booking policies](/operations/booking-policies).
- **Payment methods** control which ways to pay a member sees at checkout. See [Payment methods](/settings/payment-methods).
- **Document rules** decide which waivers and policies a member must sign before they can use their account. See [Document signatures](/content/document-signatures).
- **Plans** are what members browse and buy. See [Plans](/billing/plans).

## What members can do

Regardless of channel, members can:

- **Sign up and sign in** with email and password or a social login (Google, Facebook, Apple), then sign any required documents before their account is active.
- **Browse and book** classes, facilities (areas), and instructor sessions, join waitlists, and cancel within your policy window.
- **Buy and manage memberships** - view plans, purchase, see usage and renewal dates.
- **Pay** by card, saved card, or wallet credit, and view their invoices. See [How members pay](/billing/member-payments).
- **Manage their profile** - personal details, language, light or dark theme, and family members on their account.
- **Read messages** from your gym and reply from their inbox.
