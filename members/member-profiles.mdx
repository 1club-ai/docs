Member profiles contain all the personal information, contact details, and activity history for each club member.

## Profile Information

### Personal Details

- **Full Name** - First and last name
- **Email Address** - Primary contact email
- **Phone Number** - Contact phone number
- **Date of Birth** - Member's birth date
- **Profile Image** - Member photo

### Contact Information

- **Address** - Home address details
- **Emergency Contact** - Emergency contact information
- **Communication Preferences** - How member wants to be contacted

### Membership Information

- **Current Plan** - Active membership plan
- **Join Date** - When member joined
- **Renewal Date** - When membership expires
- **Payment Status** - Current payment status
