The Operations section manages all the day-to-day activities of your club, including bookings, classes, areas, instructors, and tasks. This is where you'll spend most of your time managing the operational aspects of your facility.

## Key Operational Areas

### Schedule Management

- **Unified Calendar View** - See all activities in one place
- **Resource Scheduling** - Manage area and instructor availability
- **Conflict Resolution** - Handle scheduling conflicts
- **Recurring Events** - Set up repeating classes and bookings

### Booking Management

- **Reservation System** - Handle area and facility bookings
- **Booking Policies** - Enforce booking rules and restrictions
- **Payment Integration** - Process booking payments
- **Cancellation Handling** - Manage booking changes and cancellations

### Class Management

- **Class Scheduling** - Create and manage group classes
- **Instructor Assignment** - Assign instructors to classes
- **Capacity Management** - Handle class enrollment and waitlists
- **Class Types** - Organize different types of classes

### Area Management

- **Facility Setup** - Configure courts, fields, and rooms
- **Area Types** - Define different types of areas
- **Pricing** - Set area-specific pricing
- **Availability** - Manage area operating hours

### Instructor Management

- **Instructor Profiles** - Manage instructor information
- **Schedule Management** - Track instructor availability
- **Performance Tracking** - Monitor instructor performance
- **Payment Processing** - Handle instructor payments

### Task Management

- **Task Creation** - Create and assign tasks
- **Progress Tracking** - Monitor task completion
- **Team Coordination** - Coordinate team activities
- **Deadline Management** - Track and manage deadlines

## Operations Workflow

### Daily Operations

1. **Morning Setup** - Review daily schedule and prepare facilities
2. **Check Bookings** - Verify all bookings and handle any issues
3. **Monitor Classes** - Ensure classes are running smoothly
4. **Handle Tasks** - Complete assigned tasks and check progress
5. **Evening Review** - Review day's activities and plan for tomorrow

### Weekly Operations

1. **Schedule Planning** - Plan upcoming week's activities
2. **Resource Allocation** - Assign areas and instructors
3. **Capacity Planning** - Ensure adequate resources are available
4. **Performance Review** - Analyze operational metrics
5. **Issue Resolution** - Address any ongoing problems

### Monthly Operations

1. **Resource Analysis** - Review area and instructor utilization
2. **Performance Metrics** - Analyze operational performance
3. **Process Improvement** - Identify areas for improvement
4. **Staff Planning** - Plan instructor schedules and assignments
5. **Facility Maintenance** - Schedule maintenance and improvements

## Key Metrics to Monitor

### Booking Metrics

- **Booking Volume** - Number of bookings per day/week/month
- **Utilization Rate** - Percentage of available time booked
- **Revenue per Booking** - Average revenue from each booking
- **Cancellation Rate** - Percentage of bookings cancelled

### Class Metrics

- **Class Attendance** - Average attendance per class
- **Class Capacity** - Utilization of class capacity
- **Instructor Performance** - Instructor ratings and feedback
- **Class Revenue** - Revenue generated from classes

### Area Metrics

- **Area Utilization** - How often each area is used
- **Peak Usage Times** - Busiest times for each area
- **Revenue per Area** - Revenue generated by each area
- **Maintenance Costs** - Costs associated with each area

### Task Metrics

- **Task Completion Rate** - Percentage of tasks completed on time
- **Task Duration** - Average time to complete tasks
- **Team Productivity** - Overall team performance
- **Issue Resolution Time** - Time to resolve operational issues

## Operational Best Practices

### Scheduling Best Practices

- **Plan Ahead** - Schedule activities well in advance
- **Buffer Time** - Allow time between bookings for setup/cleanup
- **Resource Optimization** - Maximize utilization of areas and instructors
- **Conflict Prevention** - Use system tools to prevent double-booking

### Booking Management

- **Clear Policies** - Establish and communicate booking policies
- **Easy Access** - Make booking process simple for members
- **Payment Integration** - Streamline payment processing
- **Cancellation Handling** - Have clear cancellation procedures

### Class Management

- **Consistent Scheduling** - Maintain regular class schedules
- **Capacity Planning** - Ensure adequate capacity for popular classes
- **Instructor Preparation** - Provide instructors with necessary information
- **Member Communication** - Keep members informed about class changes

### Area Management

- **Regular Maintenance** - Keep areas in good condition
- **Clear Signage** - Provide clear information about area usage
- **Safety Protocols** - Ensure safety standards are maintained
- **Equipment Management** - Keep equipment in working order

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Booking Conflicts

1. **Identify the Conflict** - Determine what's causing the conflict
2. **Check Schedules** - Verify all relevant schedules
3. **Contact Affected Parties** - Notify members of the conflict
4. **Find Alternatives** - Offer alternative times or areas
5. **Update Schedules** - Make necessary corrections

### Class Issues

1. **Monitor Attendance** - Track class participation
2. **Check Instructor Availability** - Ensure instructors are available
3. **Handle Capacity Issues** - Manage waitlists and capacity
4. **Communicate Changes** - Inform members of any changes
5. **Follow Up** - Ensure issues are resolved

### Area Problems

1. **Assess the Situation** - Determine the nature of the problem
2. **Check Maintenance Records** - Review recent maintenance
3. **Contact Maintenance** - Arrange for repairs if needed
4. **Notify Members** - Inform members of area unavailability
5. **Update Schedules** - Adjust bookings as necessary

The Operations section is designed to help you efficiently manage all aspects of your club's daily operations while maintaining high service quality and member satisfaction.
