Products are the retail items you sell alongside memberships and bookings: water and sports drinks, protein bars, apparel, gear, and any other add-on. You maintain the catalog once, and products become available to ring up on the [point of sale](/pos/point-of-sale) and to bundle into [packages](/billing/packages).

## Opening the catalog

Go to **Billing > Products** in the main menu. The URL is `/billing/products`.

The list shows each product's name, price, visibility, and active status. Click a row to open the product's detail page. Use the header buttons to add a product or to manage [categories](#categories) and [brands](#brands).

## Product fields

When you add or edit a product, you can set:

- **Name** - required.
- **Description** - shown on the product card and detail page.
- **Price** - the retail price.
- **Images** - one or more images. On the detail page you can reorder them; the first image is used as the card thumbnail.
- **Status** - active or inactive. Only active products appear on the POS.
- **Visibility** - who can see the product:
  - **Public** - visible to everyone.
  - **Members only** - visible only to signed-in members.
  - **Private** - hidden from member-facing surfaces.
- **Category** - groups the product in the catalog (see [Categories](#categories)).
- **Brand** - the maker or label (see [Brands](#brands)).
- **Features** - a short list of selling points shown on the detail page.
- **Barcode** - an optional code for the item.
- **Colour** - a swatch used to visually distinguish the product.
- **Revenue account** - the account product sales post to, for accounting. See [Billing overview](/billing/overview).

Products can be scoped to a specific location, or left available across the organization.

### Deleting a product

You can delete a product from either the list or its detail page.

## Categories

Categories group products in the catalog - for example "Drinks", "Snacks", or "Apparel". On the POS **Products** tab, products are grouped under their category heading, and anything without a category falls under "Uncategorized".

Open **Categories** from the Products page header to add, rename, delete, and reorder categories. The order you set controls the order the category headings appear in on the POS.

## Brands

Brands record the maker or label of a product. Open **Brands** from the Products page header to add, rename, delete, and reorder them, then assign a brand to a product from the product dialog.

## Stock and inventory

Products can optionally track stock. When inventory is enabled for your organization, each product's detail page gains a stock section, and you can define a recipe (the components a product consumes when sold). Selling a product on the POS then draws down stock at the location the order was placed.

<Note>
  Inventory is an optional add-on and is off by default. Stock levels, recipes,
  and stock movements are covered on the [Inventory](/pos/inventory) page.
</Note>
