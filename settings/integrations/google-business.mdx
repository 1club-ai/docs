---
title: Google Business Profile
description: Import verified business locations from Google as gyms in 1Club
---

The Google Business Profile integration pulls verified location data (name, address, phone, website) from your Google Business Profile into 1Club. You connect once and then import any of your verified locations as gyms - useful when onboarding a multi-site operator who already manages locations on Google.

## Before you start

- A Google account that owns or manages your Google Business Profile.
- At least one verified location on your Business Profile. Unverified locations are not returned by Google's API.

## Connect Google Business Profile

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** and click **Activate** on the Google Business Profile card.
2. On the integration page, click **Connect Google Business Profile**.
3. Sign in to Google and grant 1Club access to your Business Profile.
4. You are returned to 1Club with the integration marked **Active**.

## Import locations

After connecting, 1Club fetches your business accounts and the locations on the first account.

1. The **Import Multiple Locations** card lists every verified location 1Club can see.
2. Select the locations you want to import.
3. Confirm - each selected location becomes a gym in 1Club with name, address, phone, and website pre-filled from Google.

You can re-run the import later to bring in newly added locations - already-imported locations will not be duplicated.

## What syncs

This is a one-time, one-way import. Google → 1Club, on demand. Changes you make to a gym in 1Club after import do not flow back to Google, and updates you make on Google Business Profile do not automatically update the imported gym - re-run the import only if you want to pull fresh data into 1Club.

## Disconnect

Click **Disconnect** on the integration page. This revokes 1Club's access to your Business Profile. Gyms you already imported remain in 1Club untouched.

## Troubleshooting

- **"No business locations found"** - your Business Profile has no verified locations on the connected account. Verify locations in [business.google.com](https://business.google.com) and retry.
- **"OAuth authentication was denied"** - you declined the Google consent screen. Click **Connect Google Business Profile** again.
- **Wrong account connected** - disconnect, then reconnect with the Google account that manages your Business Profile.
