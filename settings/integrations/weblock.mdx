---
title: WebLock
description: Sync memberships and bookings to physical door access through WebLock (Valnes)
---

The WebLock integration ties 1Club to the Valnes WebLock physical access control system. Members and bookings in 1Club become access grants in WebLock - the right doors open at the right times without staff handing out PINs or fobs by hand. Area-to-device mappings also let you drive WebLock light rules from the booking schedule.

## Before you start

- A WebLock (Valnes) account with a **Business ID** and groups and readers already configured in the WebLock console.
- The list of groups and readers you intend to map - 1Club fetches them after you connect, but knowing what each one represents on the physical side makes mapping faster.

## Connect WebLock

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations** and click **Activate** on the WebLock card.
2. Enter your **Business ID**.
3. Adjust the two buffer values if you want (defaults are sensible):
   - **Grant Buffer Minutes** (0-120, default 15) - how long before a booking starts that access becomes valid, and how long after it ends that it remains valid.
   - **Rules Buffer Minutes** (0-120, default 5) - how long before/after a booking the area's light or device rules apply.
4. Click **Test connection** to verify the Business ID and confirm 1Club can reach WebLock.
5. Save.

Once the connection tests successfully, the **Group mapping** and **Device mapping** sections become editable.

## Group mapping

Group mapping decides which WebLock access group is granted for each entitlement in 1Club. Every mapping is optional - leave a row as **—** to skip it.

- **Plans → group** - which group active members on each plan belong to. Most operators map "Standard" plan to "Members" and so on.
- **Area type → group**, **Class type → group**, **Instructor type → group** - which group a one-off booking grants. Pick whichever axis matches how you organize doors.
- **Default group** - fallback group used for any booking that does not match a mapping above.

## Device mapping

Device mapping is independent from group mapping and drives WebLock light rules.

- **Area → reader/device** - assigns one WebLock reader to each 1Club area. When a booking starts in that area, the reader's lighting rule applies (e.g. turn on lights, indicate occupied).

Leave **—** to opt an area out of light rules.

## What syncs

- New memberships and bookings create access grants in WebLock, with a PIN generated per grant.
- Cancelling a booking or expiring a membership revokes the corresponding access grant.
- Light/device rules apply on the buffer schedule you configured.
- Access grants and the events from readers (door opened, denied) are visible under **Settings → Access Control** in 1Club.

## Disconnect

Click **Deactivate** on the integrations list. This stops 1Club from creating new grants in WebLock. Existing grants in WebLock are not retroactively removed - delete or expire them in the WebLock console if needed.

## Troubleshooting

- **Test connection fails** - the Business ID is wrong, or your WebLock account has no API access. Confirm with your WebLock administrator.
- **Group dropdowns are empty** - "No WebLock groups available. Verify the Business ID and test the connection." Re-run **Test connection** after fixing the Business ID.
- **Member can't open the door** - check **Settings → Access Control** for the access grant. If it's missing, the plan or booking may not be mapped. If it's there but `validFrom` is in the future, lower the **Grant Buffer Minutes**.
- **Lights don't come on for a booking** - the area is not mapped to a reader in **Device mapping**, or the **Rules Buffer Minutes** is larger than the time until the booking starts.
