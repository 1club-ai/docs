## What are Organization Settings?

Organization settings control the overall configuration of your club management system, including your club's information, branding, contact details, and system-wide preferences that apply to all users and members.

### **Club Information**

- **Club name** and branding

- **Business hours** and operating schedule
- **Location details** and directions
- **Legal information** and registration

### **Branding and Appearance**

- **Logo and colors** for the system

### **System Configuration**

- **Performance and monitoring** settings

## Troubleshooting

### **Common Issues**

- **Settings not saving** - Check permissions and required fields
- **Branding not updating** - Clear cache and refresh displays
- **Integration problems** - Verify API keys and configurations
- **Backup failures** - Check storage space and permissions
- **Performance issues** - Review system resources and usage

### **Configuration Problems**

- **Missing information** - Complete all required fields
- **Invalid settings** - Check format and value requirements
- **Permission errors** - Verify user access and roles
- **Integration errors** - Test connections and credentials
- **System errors** - Check logs and contact support
