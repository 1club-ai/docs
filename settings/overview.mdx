Settings is the admin area where you configure everything that is not day-to-day operations: your organization identity, clubs, members and CRM behavior, booking and POS rules, revenue and tax setup, member-facing apps, integrations, and data tools. It is organized as a left-hand category sidebar, and each category groups the related settings pages as tabs.

## Overview

- **Category sidebar** - Settings pages are grouped into eleven categories. Selecting a category shows its pages; a category with several pages exposes them as tabs across the top.
- **Permission-gated** - Most pages require an organization-level settings permission, so what you see depends on your role. See [Roles and permissions](/reference/roles-permissions).
- **Organization-scoped** - Almost every setting applies to your whole organization. Per-gym details (opening hours, address, kiosk options) live on each club instead.
- **Global search** - Settings pages and their fields are indexed, so you can jump to a specific setting from the admin search instead of hunting through categories.

## Settings categories

### Personal

Your own account, not the organization.

- **[Profile](/settings/profile)** - Your name, email, phone, photo, language, and theme.
- **Notifications** - Which inbox, task, booking, and billing notifications you receive, and on which channels.

### Organization

Organization-wide identity, access, and account.

- **[Details](/settings/organization)** - Organization name, slug, description, address, time zone, contact info, and supported locales.
- **Subscription** - Your 1Club platform plan and billing portal. This is the platform subscription (what you pay 1Club), not member billing.
- **[Users](/settings/admin-users)** - Staff accounts and their roles.
- **Permissions** - Custom permission overrides on top of the default role bundles.
- **Domains** - Custom domains for email, the CMS website, and your member portal subdomain.
- **[APIs and MCP](/settings/api-tokens)** - API tokens for programmatic access and the MCP connection.

### Clubs

Your locations and the shared vocabulary used to categorize spaces, staff, and classes.

- **All** - Add and edit club locations (the club list).
- **Area types** - Bookable space categories such as Tennis Court or Reformer Studio. Individual courts and rooms are created as [areas](/operations/areas) per club.
- **Instructor types** - Instructor categories such as Personal Trainer or Yoga Instructor.
- **[Class types](/operations/class-types)** - Class categories, including auto-cancellation behavior when nobody books.

### Access control

- **Settings** - Identification methods for entry: QR code, access card, and PIN code.
- **Grants** - Which memberships, plans, or groups are granted physical access.

See [Access control](/settings/access-control) for the full flow.

### CRM

How contacts, leads, tags, and progression behave.

- **Contacts** - Contact fields and which are required, optional, or hidden.
- **[Forms](/settings/signup-forms)** - Public signup form configuration.
- **[Deals](/marketing/deals)** - Deal pipeline fields, sources, and stages.
- **[Smart tagging](/settings/smart-tagging)** - Rule-based tags applied automatically (engagement, financial, lifecycle, membership, level, age, interest, scoring).
- **Manual tagging** - Tags you apply by hand.
- **Progression levels** - Skill or belt progression tracks for members.
- **[Matches](/members/matches)** - Rating settings for competitive play and matchmaking.

### Operations

The rules the schedule and front desk run on.

- **[Booking policies](/operations/booking-policies)** - When payment is enforced, whether check-in is required, cancellation refunds, lead times, and slot limits.
- **Waivers** - Documents members must sign and the rules that require them.
- **Schedule display** - Coloring and visibility rules for the schedule.
- **Point of Sale** - Enable the POS terminal and connect a card reader.
- **Inventory management** - Stock tracking configuration for products.

### Revenue

Financial configuration for member billing. (Referred to as **Revenue** in the sidebar; the underlying route is `/billing`.)

- **General** - Currency, invoice numbering template, tax number, and whether prices show with tax. This page also holds tax rates, revenue accounts, [payment methods](/settings/payment-methods), and [external programs](/settings/external-programs).
- **Pricing rules** - Automatic discounts and surcharges applied at checkout.
- **Automated billing** - The scheduler that generates recurring invoices ([bill runs](/billing/bill-runs)).

### Member apps

Branding and the member-facing channels.

Members sign in to every member-facing app with **Google**, **Apple**, **Facebook**, or an email and password. The same account works on the web and in the mobile app. See [Signing in](/reference/sign-in).

- **Branding** - Logo, favicon, colors (light and dark), and typography. Requires a Grow or Run plan.
- **Member portal** - Enable the portal and choose which pages members see.
- **Mobile app** - App content and store links.
- **Kiosk app** - Self-service kiosk features, configured per club.

The 1club.me marketplace listing is managed under [Marketplace](/marketing/marketplace) in Marketing, not in Settings.

### Communication and AI

- **Templates** - Reusable document and email templates.
- **Email inboxes** - Inbound email configuration for shared inboxes.
- **AI agents** - Configure the AI assistant and example prompts.
- **Conversation history** - Review past AI conversations.

### Integrations

- **[Integrations](/settings/integrations)** - Stripe, Google Calendar, Google Business Profile, Weblock (access control), and Google Tag Manager.

### Data tools

- **Import** - Bring in members, contacts, and other records.
- **[Export](/settings/export)** - Export organization data as CSV or prepare it for QuickBooks.
- **Archive** - Archive and data retention.

## Where common tasks live

| I want to... | Go to |
| --- | --- |
| Rename the organization or change its portal address | **Organization > Details** |
| Change my 1Club plan or payment method for 1Club | **Organization > Subscription** |
| Add a new gym location | **Clubs > All** |
| Change logo and brand colors | **Member Apps > Branding** |
| Set who pays when and how cancellations refund | **Operations > Booking policies** |
| Set the currency and tax number on invoices | **Revenue > General** |
| Connect Stripe | **Integrations** |
| Add a custom website or email domain | **Organization > Domains** |

## Tips and best practices

- **Configure top-down.** Set the organization details and time zone first, then add clubs, then branding and member apps. Downstream pages (schedule, invoices, exports) inherit the organization time zone and currency.
- **Let roles do the gating.** Rather than sharing one admin login, invite staff under **Organization > Users** with the narrowest role that fits. Front desk, instructor, and manager roles each expose a different slice of Settings.
- **Keep member billing separate from your 1Club bill.** **Revenue** is how you charge members; **Organization > Subscription** is how 1Club charges you. They are unrelated.
- **Use search for deep settings.** Individual fields (for example, invoice numbering template or kiosk options) are indexed, so searching is faster than clicking through categories.

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | Cause | Fix |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A Settings category or page is missing from your sidebar | Your role lacks the required organization settings permission | Ask an Admin to grant it, or check [Roles and permissions](/reference/roles-permissions) |
| Branding page blocks saving with an upgrade message | Branding customization requires a Grow or Run plan | Upgrade under **Organization > Subscription** |
| Booking policy or tax setting is not taking effect | It is scoped to a specific club, area type, or class type | Open the setting and confirm its scope matches the bookings you expect it to affect |
| Cannot find the marketplace listing in Settings | The listing moved to Marketing | Open [Marketplace](/marketing/marketplace) |
