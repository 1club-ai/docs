**In a nutshell:** Create, schedule, and manage fitness classes with instructor assignments, capacity limits, and member booking capabilities.

## What is Class Management?

Class management handles everything related to your fitness classes, from creation and scheduling to instructor assignments and member bookings. It ensures your classes run smoothly and members can easily find and book the sessions they want.

## Key Features

### **Class Creation**

- **Define class types** and descriptions
- **Set capacity limits** and requirements
- **Assign instructors** and equipment needs
- **Create recurring** class schedules

### **Schedule Management**

- **Weekly schedules** for regular classes
- **Special events** and workshops
- **Holiday schedules** and closures
- **Schedule changes** and updates

### **Booking Management**

- **Member booking** and cancellation
- **Waitlist management** for full classes
- **Class capacity** monitoring
- **Attendance tracking** and check-in
